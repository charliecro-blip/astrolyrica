# Database Entry Expansion Prompts

Use these prompts to expand AstroLyrica YAML entries in original language. Never copy source prose or imitate a modern author's distinctive style.

## Planet entry

Expand the planet `{planet_name}` with original symbolic language. Return YAML fields for `traditional_keywords`, `poetic_images`, `verbs`, `body_language`, `shadow_language`, `medicine_language`, and `avoid`. Emphasize reusable images, textures, actions, and interpretive logic.

## Sign entry

Expand the sign `{sign_name}` with original symbolic language. Return YAML fields for `element`, `modality`, `temperament`, `body_zone`, `poetic_images`, `verbs`, `gift_language`, `risk_language`, and `avoid`. Keep the sign complex and avoid stereotypes.

## House entry

Expand the house `{house_name}` with original symbolic language. Return YAML fields for `traditional_topics`, `modern_topics`, `poetic_locations`, `verbs`, `shadow_language`, and `medicine_language`. Include concrete rooms, thresholds, tools, and social settings.

## Aspect entry

Expand the aspect `{aspect_name}` with original symbolic language. Return YAML fields for `angle`, `poetic_logic`, `sentence_patterns`, `constructive_use`, and `avoid`. Describe relationship dynamics between symbols without flattening the aspect into good or bad.

## Condition entry

Expand the condition `{condition_name}` with original symbolic language. Return YAML fields for `technical_meaning`, `poetic_images`, `emotional_tone`, `interpretive_rules`, and `avoid`. Distinguish technical doctrine from poetic metaphor.

## Review checklist

- Is every phrase original?
- Are there concrete nouns and active verbs?
- Are avoid lists specific enough to prevent clichés?
- Could the entry be recombined across poems, horoscopes, and image prompts?
